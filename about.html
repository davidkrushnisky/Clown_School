Clowns R Us

We want to bring laughter and joy into the world as well as promote extraordinary theatrical classes and events to adults and children alike. We anchor our work in the discipline of clown because we believe that clowning embraces and celebrates the totality of human experience and offers unparalleled freedom in art. We love to play. We have 6 main team members as well as teaching staff. Learn more about them here!

We offer the highest quality of live and virtual clown training to clowns, actors, performers,comedians, and participants from all walks of life, including doctors, teachers, healers, film-makers,and students. .
